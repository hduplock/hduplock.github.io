About the Havanese

Havanese are wonderful dogs and great for families even with young children and other animals. Here are some of their personality traits:

  • Playful
  • Affectionate
  • Intelligent
  • Responsive
  • Companionable
  • Gentle

Here is some basic information about the breed:

  • The life span is 13-15 years
  • They are around 9 inches tall (when on all fours and measured from paw to shoulder)
  • They can weigh 10-16 lbs

As with all dogs, there are some health problems you can expect:

  • Luxating patella
  • liver disease
  • heart disease
  • Cataracts

Overall Havanese are wonderful dogs. They are great with kids, families, and people of all ages. They get along well with any animal and just want to be friends with everything. Just make sure you keep them nice and groomed

All information courtesy of American Kennel Club And Wikipedia
